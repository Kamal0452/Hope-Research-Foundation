INSTRUTIONS

1. We admit that we are powerless over alcohol. We couldn't live life properly.
2. We come to believe that a power greater than ourselves can provide us with clarity.
3. We were able to commit our will and life to the responsibility of the Lord we understood.
4. We fearlessly prepared an in-depth character list about ourselves.
5. We admit the true nature of our faults to God, to ourselves, and to one another.
6. We prepared ourselves wholeheartedly to remove all these character defects from us.
7. We humbly asked God to remove our shortcomings.
8. We made a list of whom we had wronged and prepared to make amends.
9. We did direct redress as far as possible so as not to harm our victims or others.
10.Continuously self-reflected and readily admitted when we made mistakes.
11. Through prayer and meditation we sought to develop an inner connection with the Lord we understood, only to know what His will was for us and to pray for the power to accomplish it.
12. As a result of following these methods we got spiritual awakening. We tried to exemplify these principles to other patients and try to apply these principles in all aspects of our lives.
